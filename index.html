About Me

I am a lecturer at the Computer Systems Engineering Program, Universitas Muhammadiyah Buton, with a focus on Medical Imaging, Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and the Internet of Things (IoT). My academic background includes a Bachelor's degree in Informatics Engineering from Universitas Brawijaya and a Master's degree in Information Technology from Universitas Gadjah Mada.

In my professional development, I actively participate in various international certifications such as the TensorFlow Developer Specialization by DeepLearning.AI, as well as national AI training programs conducted by the Indonesian Ministry of Communication and Informatics in collaboration with Universitas Indonesia through the Digital Talent Scholarship. Additionally, I have experience as a facilitator and instructor for school teachers in programming and artificial intelligence programs organized by the Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology for primary and secondary education levels.

My expertise focuses on applying AI and IoT technologies in the context of education, healthcare, and the environment using a research-driven and practical approach. I am also currently exploring geospatial analysis and mapping using QGIS, particularly in relation to community development and environmental monitoring. As an educator, I am committed to mentoring students and teachers in developing impactful and applicable technological solutions.
